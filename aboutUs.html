About Lore.Jo


Founding

In the ever-increasing demand for deep lore for games and media, Mr Squiggles (Dave Smith), founded “Lore.jo” in his garage to help sell and develop e-books that explores the mysteries of deep lore within fictional worlds. Over time the business became more and more popular, striking a chord with people that loved to be immersed into the deepest parts of their favourite universes.


Business

Lore.jo provides in-depth and detailed background lores of games and media in an e-book format. Lore.jo also provides revisions of books that the details are incorrect. These revisions can be sold at a discounted price if the owner owns the original. Additional volumes of e-book will be produced if the game or media franchise is expanded upon by the developers and creator.


Pride

The company prides itself on maintaining and ensuring that all details within the e-books are as accurate and as detailed as possible. We often encourage fans to contact us with details and information regarding their favourite franchises to help us develop the books. The company even goes to lengths to create “dive teams” of staff that will envelop themselves in the franchise and explore every deepest bit of lore that there is to find.


Products

The types of products that Lore.jo will all be provided in downloadable e-book format. This includes the volumes and revision when deemed that the original needs to be corrected or expanded upon.The e-books produced are guaranteed to be as accurate and detailed as the time of writing. If a product is proven to be incorrect or outdated, the person proving such will be provided with a free copy of the planned revision
