Browsing Products

You can use the search on the navigation bar to search up ebooks by keywords. Press enter.



Then, you will be brought to this page where you can see all the products that matches the keywords.




You can also see the top rated, on sale, and all of the products on the home page.
To access the homepage, press the home icon or logo on the navigation bar.





Adding items to cart

To add to cart, simply press the add to cart button.
This can be found on the product card or the product page


Viewing your cart

To view your cart simply press the cart button on the navigation bar.



This where you can clear your cart, remove items, and checkout.


Account

To log in or sign up. Click the user icon on the navigation bar.
